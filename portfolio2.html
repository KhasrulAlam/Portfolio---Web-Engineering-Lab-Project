Application of some supervised learning algorithm in Statlog Data Set


In this project, we have taken a dataset regarding heart diseases. There were 270 instances and 13 attributes. Where each instance represents a human and attributes are about different healthiness measurements like age, sex, chest pain, cholesterol, heartbeat rate, blood sugar rate etc. and a class attribute which tells if this person has heart disease or not. We have applied 4 different classifiers to our dataset. Our objective is to analyze and compare the performance of above -mentioned classifiers using ROC (receiver operating characteristic) curve, ROC Graph and finding the most suitable classifier. Those 4 classifiers used by us are : J48, Random Tree, ZeroR, Navie Bayes



Unsupervised Learning on Absenteeism at work Dataset


We have taken a dataset regarding Absenteeism at work. There were 720 instances and 21 attributes. Where each instance represents an employee of an office and attributes are ID, Reason for absence Absenteeism_time_in_hours, Month of absence, Day of the week, Seasons, Transportation expense, Distance from Residence to Work, Service time, Age, Workload, Average/day, Hit the target, Disciplinary failure, Education, Son, Social drinker, Social smoker, Pet, Weight, Height, Body mass index which can be related for causing their absence in office at different time. We have applied a hierarchical clustering algorithm to our dataset. Our objective is simply to cluster the reasons behind employees' absence and find a correlation between reasons and suggest ways to minimize absences percentage.
